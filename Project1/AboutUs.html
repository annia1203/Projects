WHAT WE DO


From price transparency to artwork authenticity, there are many advantages of buying art at auction.


Art is too important not to share

Established in 2001, Kalopsia is the world’s most trusted and dynamic marketplace for art. We empower our international community of collectors to discover and acquire fine art. In here you will find master pieces from our favorite periods and techniques, which include Classic Art, Contemporary, Modern and Digital Art. Guided by our forward-thinking spirit of innovation, we offer a cross-category selection of items available for immediate purchase via both digital and physical shopping experiences as well as private sales.
